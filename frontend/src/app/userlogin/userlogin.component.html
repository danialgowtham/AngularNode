Hinduja Tech (earlier 'Defiance Technologies') is a Hinduja Group Company incorporated in 2009, with a focus to provide Engineering, Manufacturing and Enterprise (EME) Services and Solutions for Automotive, Aerospace, Defense, Industrial and General Manufacturing industries. Hinduja Tech provides Integrated Engineering, Manufacturing and Enterprise Solutions to Automotive and Manufacturing Customers across Globe. It is led by a management team with global experience in delivering high-end solutions in Design, Engineering, ERP and IT services. As a business solution focused company, Hinduja Tech has established Centres of Excellence (CoE) to develop solutions to address key customer business imperatives in the Product Engineering, Manufacturing and Enterprise domains.
